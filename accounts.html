Hold This - Data Deletion Request

If you want to delete your account and any associated data for the game Hold This, please note:

  • The game uses your Google Play Games account for login, so your game progress is linked to your Google account.
  • To delete your game data, you need to delete the app data stored on your device or revoke the app's access in your Google Account settings.
  • You can also contact Google Play support to request deletion of any stored game data linked to your account.
  • No personal selfies or screenshots are stored or shared by the app unless you explicitly choose to share them.

Steps to delete your data:

  1. Uninstall the app to remove all local data on your device.
  2. Go to your Google Account permissions page: https://myaccount.google.com/permissions and remove the app's access if you want to revoke permissions.
  3. Contact Google Play Support if you want to request deletion of any stored data on Google servers.
